Socioeconomic Irrigation
Ending poverty is within our grasp. It used to seem impossible to guarantee that crops growing inland could receive their optimum amount of water, sun, shade, and soil nutrition. This is something we take for granted for how normal it is now.
Irrigation was invented between 6,000-3,100 BCE.
We have the same opportunity now, to make sure every human receives all needs. AoC creates the structure to ensure that by viewing money as the water we all need and then channeling it from abundant sources and channeling it to fields of need.
Who knows what amazing beauty, innovation, creative endeavors, and joy we can collectively experience and appreciate if we had this structure and shift? I’m desperately curious to know. Let’s find out.
